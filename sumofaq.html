SumoBot FAQ

Does it work on Lunar?

Sumobot is a Forge mod, meaning 1.8.9 Forge will need to be installed, and the sumobot JAR will need to be places into your mod folder.

Does it log my MC username or password?

Nope! This mod does not log your password at all! And it only uses your MC username, to send webhooks to your own custom webhook on discord, or to search up your current sumo stats.

Does it work without cheats?

Yes! Everything in the bot is highly customizable, and it can still win the majority of games without any other modifications.

What payment methods do you accept.

We currently only accept: $10 Paypal, $10 BTC, 1000 Hypixel Gold (Not always accepted).

I sent the money via paypal, when will I get access?

Payments may take up to 24 hours for paypal to register. As well as this, it may take a day for you to be authenticated via the admins.

Can you add "X" or "Y" to the bot?

You can submit suggestions for the bot in our Discord! (https://discord.gg/UXwgDXxcBe)

May I share the JAR file?

The mod file requires a token to use. While this prevents anyone but you from using the bot, it is still forbidden to distribute our files.

Can I share the bot?

Sharing the account is strictly forbidden, and your account will be suspended.

Why isnt the bot working?

You may not have installed the nexessary files needed to run the bot. These need to be in your mod folder, along with the sumobot mod. Download them here: Optifine & Patcher.
