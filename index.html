What Is A Sumobot?
This is a 1.8.9 forge mod, that when activated will automate the process of winning sumo games for you. It will queue, fight and either win or lose, warping into another game and re-doing the process. This bot has tons of customizability, such as custom Aim Assist Speed, CPS, and more! This bot uses complex maths to calculate optimal strafing patterns, WTap timings and sprint jumps, to make sure you have the best chance of winning games.
Sumobot Features
Current Version [0.7] - Features
24/09/21
-    Auto Queue :
Automatically queues sumo games for you, with built in fail safes for limbo and server kicks. You'll never have to manually queue again!
-    WTap :
Deals more knockback to your opponent by taping W for a period of time after hitting them!
-    Queue Dodging :
Dodges players that have too high of a WLR or Winstreak! This is to make sure you have the highest chance of winning your games.
-    Fast Queue :
A new sped up way of queueing, leaving and rejoining whenever an opponent leaves to speed up your progress!
-    Auto Throw :
Lately, Hypixel staff have been banning suspicious players because of their winstreak. This feature intentionally losses some games to seem more legit!
-    Discord Webook :
Sends a message to a discord webhook every win, loss and dodge to keep you updated on your bots actions!
-    Auto Disconnect :
Disconnect after a certain amount of wins, so you can make sure you dont go on leaderboards while you AFK! This is also good for seeming legit!
-    Dodge Nicks/IGN :
Allows you to dodge nicked players, or a list of players you can add to! No more going up against tryhards, or unknown players!
